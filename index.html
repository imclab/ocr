Reading handwriting with an Applet and Deep Neural Networks

Your browser is completely ignoring the <applet> tag!

Wait for the Applet to load and you will be able to draw a letter or digit using your mouse. This character will be recognized in real time by a Neural Network trained using Deep Learning.

Deep Learning is a new area of Machine Learning research, which has been introduced with the objective of moving Machine Learning closer to one of its original goals: Artificial Intelligence.
